About the Zodiac Calendar


This calendar is a reform of the Gregorian calendar, designed to synchronize more naturally with the solar year and the astronomical zodiac.


Structure

  • 13 Months: Each month has exactly 28 days (4 weeks).
  • Sol Month: An extra month at the end of the year containing 1 day (or 2 in leap years) to complete the 365/366 day cycle.
  • Start Date: The year begins on the Winter Solstice (December 22nd).

Months

The months are named after the astronomical constellations the sun passes through:

  1. Capricorn
  2. Aquarius
  3. Pisces
  4. Aries
  5. Taurus
  6. Gemini
  7. Cancer
  8. Leo
  9. Virgo
  10. Libra
  11. Scorpio
  12. Ophiuchus (The 13th Sign)
  13. Sagittarius
  14. Sol (The Sun / Void Month)
